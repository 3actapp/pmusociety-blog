### Our Ad Strategies: Precision Targeting for Maximum Impact

We employ a multifaceted approach to paid advertising, leveraging the strengths of each platform to create a comprehensive and highly effective campaign for your PMU business.

#### Facebook and Instagram Ads: Engaging Your Ideal Client

Facebook and Instagram are visual platforms, making them perfect for showcasing the transformative power of permanent makeup. Our strategies focus on:

*   **High Impact Visuals:** We create compelling ad creatives featuring before and after photos, client testimonials, and engaging video content that captures attention and builds trust.
*   **Hypertargeted Audiences:** We delve deep into demographic, interest, and behavioural targeting to reach individuals most likely to be interested in PMU services. This includes targeting based on interests like beauty, aesthetics, skincare, and even specific competitor followings.
*   **Retargeting Campaigns:** We implement sophisticated retargeting strategies to reengage potential clients who have previously shown interest in your services, ensuring no lead is left behind.

#### Google Ads: Capturing Intent Driven Searches

Google Ads allows us to capture clients at the very moment they are searching for PMU services. Our approach includes:

*   **Keyword Domination:** We identify and target high intent keywords such as "microblading near me", "lip blush London", or "permanent eyeliner UK", ensuring your business appears at the top of search results.
*   **Local SEO Integration:** We optimise your Google My Business profile and integrate it with your ad campaigns to drive local traffic and bookings.
*   **Compelling Ad Copy:** Our ad copy is crafted to highlight your unique selling propositions, encouraging immediate clicks and enquiries.

### Measuring Success: Our Commitment to ROI

Transparency and measurable results are at the core of our operations. We provide detailed reports on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as:

*   **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** How much it costs to acquire a new client.
*   **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** The revenue generated for every pound spent on advertising.
*   **Booking Conversion Rates:** The percentage of ad clicks that result in a confirmed booking.

Our goal is not just to generate clicks, but to deliver a significant return on your investment, ensuring your advertising budget is working as hard as possible for your business.
