## The Single Ad Funnel: Your Client Machine

Forget what you have heard about complicated, multi step funnels that require a degree in computer science to build. The beauty of advertising for a local service business like a PMU studio is its simplicity. You do not need a 27 step email sequence or a webinar. You need one simple, effective funnel that turns cold prospects into booked appointments.

We call it the Single Ad Funnel, and it has just three core components.

### Step 1: The Ad Itself

This is your digital billboard. Your goal is to stop the scroll and grab the attention of your ideal client. We run these ads on Facebook and Instagram using the Lead Generation objective. This allows potential clients to submit their information directly on the platform, making it incredibly easy for them to show interest.

*   **Targeting:** Keep it simple and local. We target women, typically aged 25 to 55, who live within a 10 to 15 mile radius of your studio. We then layer interests like beauty, cosmetics, skincare, and luxury goods. Facebook’s algorithm is smarter than you think; give it a clear target and it will find the right people.
*   **Creative:** This is your most important asset. High quality video is king. A short clip showing your studio, your process, and the incredible transformation of a client works wonders. If you do not have video, a carousel of your absolute best before and after photos is the next best thing. People need to see the results to believe in the investment.
*   **Copy:** Speak directly to their desires. The copy should be benefit driven, not feature driven. Nobody is buying ‘microblading’; they are buying the confidence of waking up with perfect brows every single day. Use clear, compelling language that focuses on the outcome. A strong call to action like "Tap Get Quote for our latest pricing and availability" is essential.

### Step 2: The Instant Lead Form

When a user clicks your ad, they are presented with a simple form. The key here is to minimise friction. Ask for the absolute minimum information you need to follow up. This is typically just their Name, Email, and Phone Number. Every extra field you add will reduce the number of people who complete the form. Make it easy for them to say yes.

### Step 3: The Automated Follow Up Sequence

This is the secret sauce. This is where 99% of PMU artists fail. A lead is useless if you do not have a system to convert it. As soon as that lead form is submitted, the clock is ticking. You must follow up instantly. Not in an hour. Not when you finish with your current client. Instantly.

This is where a dedicated CRM becomes essential. The moment a lead comes in, it should trigger an automated sequence of text messages and emails. The goal of this sequence is not to sell them on the spot; it is to get them to book a consultation call or appointment.

Here is a sample of a proven sequence:

*   **SMS 1 (Immediate):** "Hey [First Name], it's [Your Name] from [Your Studio]. Thanks for your interest in our brow treatments! Are you free for a quick chat sometime this week to discuss your goals?"
*   **Email 1 (5 minutes later):** A more detailed email with information about your services, pricing, and a link to your booking page.
*   **SMS 2 (24 hours later):** "Hi [First Name], just wanted to follow up on my message from yesterday. We have a couple of consultation spots open on Thursday and Friday if you'd like to book one. You can see availability here: [Booking Link]"

This automated system works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring that every single lead gets a prompt, professional follow up, dramatically increasing the chance they will book.
