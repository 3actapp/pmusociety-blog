## Showcase Your Expertise with Pride

The first step to attracting a higher calibre of client is to position yourself as an undeniable expert. Premium clients are not looking for the cheapest option. They are looking for the best. They want the reassurance that they are in the safest, most skilled hands. How do you provide that reassurance? You show off your credentials and certifications. Do not be shy about it. 

Display your certificates prominently in your studio. Create a dedicated page on your website that details your training, your qualifications, and any masterclasses you have attended. When you post on social media, mention your commitment to professional development. This is not about bragging. It is about building trust and demonstrating your value. A client who sees that you have invested heavily in your education is far more likely to understand why your services command a higher price. They are paying for your expertise, your knowledge, and your commitment to excellence, not just the time you spend with them.

Think about it from their perspective. They are trusting you with their face. They want to know you are a master of your craft, not a hobbyist who took a weekend course. Your certifications are your proof. They are a powerful signal to the market that you are a serious professional who delivers exceptional results. So, frame them, digitise them, and broadcast them. Let your qualifications do the selling for you.

## Curate a VIP Experience from Start to Finish

Premium clients expect a premium experience. They are not just buying a treatment. They are buying a feeling. They want to feel pampered, valued, and special from the moment they first interact with your brand to the moment they walk out of your studio with their flawless new brows or lips. This is where you can truly set yourself apart from the competition. 

Consider every single touchpoint of the client journey. Is your booking process seamless and professional? Our Scale My Salon CRM, for example, automates this, ensuring every client receives timely confirmations and reminders. What happens when they walk through your door? Are they greeted with a warm smile and offered a gourmet coffee or a glass of prosecco? Is your studio space immaculately clean, beautifully decorated, and filled with luxurious touches like plush blankets and scented candles? These small details make a huge difference. They create an atmosphere of exclusivity and care that high paying clients are drawn to.

The VIP treatment should extend to the consultation itself. Take the time to listen to their concerns, understand their desires, and explain your proposed treatment plan in detail. This is not a rushed, transactional conversation. It is a collaborative process that makes the client feel heard and understood. By creating this luxe, personalised experience, you are justifying your premium price point at every turn. You are showing them that a treatment with you is an investment in themselves, a luxury experience they will not find anywhere else.

## Educate Your Clients on the Why

Many artists make the mistake of assuming clients understand the intricacies of permanent makeup. They do not. It is your job to educate them. When you take the time to explain the why behind your methods, your tools, and your pricing, you transform from a service provider into a trusted advisor. This is a crucial shift in perception. 

During the consultation, explain why you use a particular pigment line, why your technique is superior for their skin type, or why a specific aftercare routine is essential for optimal results. Share your knowledge generously. When a client understands the level of skill, precision, and quality materials involved, the price becomes a secondary consideration. They are no longer just paying for the outcome. They are paying for the peace of mind that comes with knowing they are in the hands of a true expert who does not cut corners.

This educational approach also helps to filter out clients who are purely price driven. A client who is only interested in the cheapest deal will not value a detailed explanation of your process. A premium client, however, will appreciate your transparency and expertise. They want to understand the value they are receiving. By educating them, you empower them to make an informed decision, and you build a foundation of trust that can last for years. This is how you build a loyal clientele that returns again and again.

## Filter Out the Time Wasters

One of the biggest drains on a PMU artist's time and energy is dealing with people who are not serious about booking. They ask endless questions, demand discounts, and then disappear without a trace. Or worse, they book an appointment and then fail to show up. To attract premium clients, you must have systems in place to filter out these time wasters. The most effective way to do this is by implementing deposits and consultation fees.

A non refundable deposit for treatment bookings is non negotiable. It demonstrates that the client is serious and committed, and it protects you from lost income due to no shows. Do not be afraid to implement this. A serious client will have no problem paying a deposit to secure their spot. A time waster will.

Furthermore, consider charging a fee for your consultations. Your time and expertise are valuable. A consultation is not just a casual chat. It is a professional assessment where you provide personalised advice and a detailed treatment plan. Charging a fee for this service immediately elevates its perceived value and ensures you are only spending your precious time with potential clients who are genuinely interested in moving forward. You can always choose to apply the consultation fee towards the final treatment cost if they book with you, creating a win win situation. These small financial commitments act as powerful filters, leaving you with a calendar full of serious, respectful, and high quality clients.

Invest in advanced masterclasses with the industry's best trainers. Travel to conferences. Join professional organisations. This demonstrates your passion and your dedication to your craft, further solidifying your status as a leading expert in your area. Remember, your skills are your greatest asset. The more you sharpen them, the more valuable they become.
