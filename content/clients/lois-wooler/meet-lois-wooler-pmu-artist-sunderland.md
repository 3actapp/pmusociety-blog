## A Full Range of Permanent Makeup Services

![Semi-permanent makeup treatment at LoW Makeup & Microblading](/images/clients/lois-wooler/lois-spmu-treatment.webp)
*Every treatment at LoW is performed with precision and care, using high-quality sterile equipment.*

Lois offers a comprehensive suite of semi-permanent makeup treatments, each delivered with the same level of care and precision:

### Powder Brows and Ombré Brows

Powder brows give a soft, shaded finish that mimics the look of brow makeup — but wakes up with you every morning. Lois's powder brow work is consistently praised for looking natural and polished rather than heavy or drawn-on. Ideal for clients who want defined, full-looking brows without the daily effort.

### Microblading

Microblading uses fine, hair-like strokes to create the most natural-looking brow result possible. It is perfect for clients with sparse or uneven brows who want a realistic, feathered finish. Lois has performed hundreds of microblading treatments and her results consistently look like real hair growth.

### Combination Brows

Combination brows blend microblading at the front of the brow with powder shading through the body and tail. The result is definition, depth, and fullness — the best of both techniques in one treatment. This is one of Lois's most popular services.

### Lip Blush

Lip blush is one of the most transformative treatments in the PMU world. It enhances the natural colour and shape of the lips, adds definition, and creates a youthful, healthy-looking pout that lasts. Lois's lip blush work is particularly sought after for its soft, buildable colour and precise outline work.

### Tattoo Eyeliner

From a subtle lash enhancement to a more defined eyeliner look, Lois's eyeliner tattooing is clean, precise, and long-lasting. It is a game changer for clients who wear eyeliner daily or who struggle with application due to conditions like arthritis or visual impairment.

### Scalp Micropigmentation

Perhaps the most life-changing service Lois offers, scalp micropigmentation (SMP) creates the appearance of a shaved head or denser hair for clients experiencing hair loss or thinning. Lois has helped clients with medical hair loss conditions regain their confidence through this treatment, and the emotional impact of her work in this area is something she speaks about with genuine passion.

### Mobile Clinic and Annual Boost Appointments

Lois also offers mobile clinic services, bringing her expertise directly to clients who cannot travel. And for existing clients, her annual boost appointments ensure results stay fresh and vibrant year after year.
